Who are we?

We are an organization of Alfresco community members working to grow the Alfresco ecosystem independent of Alfresco Software, Inc.

We are all different: some of us are paying customers; some work for partners; and some are Alfresco Software employees. Some of us work with Alfresco Community Edition while others work with Alfresco Enterprise Edition, or both. Other members use Alfresco as end users, implement it for customers, and develop free and paid add-ons. But we have something in common: we like Alfresco and its community and we think that Alfresco Community Edition and open source solutions based on it are very important to the whole market.

Our mission is to guarantee the existence of Alfresco Community Edition as a free/libre open source solution for document management.

What are we doing?


Alfresco Honeycomb Edition

Alfresco Honeycomb Edition is based on Alfresco Community Edition and includes a combination of scripts, content models, workflows, extensions and patches.

Alfresco Honeycomb Edition includes some of the most popular and useful add-ons that are available as well as a number of scripts that can be used to install Alfresco more quickly.

Tutorials and Articles

We monitor technical Alfresco-related blogs and add the most helpful tutorials, articles and posts to our archive.

If you do not know how to use the Maven SDK or how to create workflows, welcome to our archive. Here you can find small hacks and tips that can be useful during Alfresco implementation, and stories that our members want to share.

Professional Services Network

Many companies are looking for a team or a person who can implement a project based on Alfresco Community Edition. Or perhaps they need help to develop an extension or solve some specific task. To address this, we are forming a list of well-known systems integrators and freelancers all over the world who can help you.
